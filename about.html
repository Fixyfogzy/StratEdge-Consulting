Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a leading consulting firm in Nigeria

Pioneering Business Excellence Since 2010

StratEdge Consulting was founded in Lagos, Nigeria, with a simple yet powerful vision: to help businesses navigate the complexities of the modern market and achieve sustainable growth. Our journey began with just three consultants and a shared passion for strategic problem-solving.

Over the past decade, we've grown into a team of over 50 experienced professionals, serving clients across various industries. Our success is built on a foundation of deep industry knowledge, innovative thinking, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' success.

Today, we're proud to be recognized as one of Nigeria's most trusted consulting partners, helping businesses transform challenges into opportunities and strategies into tangible results.

Our Approach to Consulting

At StratEdge, we believe that every business is unique, with its own set of challenges and opportunities. That's why we take a customized approach to every engagement, developing tailored solutions that address your specific needs.

Our methodology combines rigorous analysis with creative thinking, ensuring that our recommendations are both data-driven and innovative. We work collaboratively with your team, building capabilities and transferring knowledge to ensure lasting impact.

Whether you're looking to enter new markets, optimize operations, or drive digital transformation, our team has the expertise and experience to guide you every step of the way.
